APPERANCE

Narcissus-jonquilla is a bulbous flowering plant, a specie (one out of  10.000) of Narcissus (daffodil). The flower can be  yellow, orange or white. Its leaves are long and narrow.

PARENTAGE

Narcissus-jonquilla is native to Spain and Portugal, but we can see it in France, Italy, Turkey, Madra, the U.S  too, because the flower naturalized in many countries. 

CULTIVATION

People cultivate Narcissus-jonquilla in France for the Narcissus Oil, a component of many modern perfumes.
