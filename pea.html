PEA

The pea is a small sphericall seed or a seed-pod of the pod fruit Pisum sativum.






NEUTRITIONAL VALUE

Peas have vitamin A, vitamin B6, vitamin C, vitamin K, phosphorus, magnesium, copper, iron, zinc and lutein. 

MODERN CULINARY USE

Peas  are  usually boiled or steamed (Middle East, North Africa, Europe-Middle Ages). Green Peas  became very popular in France (XVII, XVIII). 

PEA 
IN SCIENCE

G.Mendel  observed pea pods. These observations  led to principles of Mendelian   Genetics.

FESTIVAL
